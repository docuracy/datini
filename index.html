About this dataset

145,417 letters from the Archivio di Stato di Prato, spanning the Mediterranean trade network of the Datini company c. 1363–1412 and geocoded to 285 cities.

Citation

Franklin-Lyons & Oleinikov (2025). Datini Letter Collection Metadata. Digital Philology 14(2): 384–391.

Dataset

OSF · Datini Correspondence Metadata Project

Letter editions
